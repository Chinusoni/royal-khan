Who We Are

Established as a trusted name in the industrial sector, Royal Khan Company is a premier manufacturer, exporter, and supplier of heavy-duty packaging solutions. Operating out of Delhi, we have spent over a decade perfecting the art and engineering of packaging machinery.

We specialize in a comprehensive range of equipment, from Continuous Band Sealers and Cap Sealing Machines to automated Liquid Filling Machines. Our products are the backbone of production lines across the Dairy, Food & Beverage, Pharmaceutical, and Chemical industries.

"Our goal is simple: to provide robust, low-maintenance, and high-efficiency machines that empower businesses to grow without worrying about operational bottlenecks."
