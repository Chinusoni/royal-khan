ABOUT ROYAL KHAN

A Decade of Packaging Excellence

Founded in Delhi, Royal Khan Company has grown from a small workshop into one of India's most recognized names in industrial packaging machinery. We specialize in the design, manufacture, and supply of continuous band sealing machines, induction cap sealers, liquid filling machines, and ancillary packaging equipment.

Our manufacturing philosophy centers on three pillars: engineering precision, material quality, and honest pricing. Every machine undergoes a 24-point quality inspection before dispatch. We use food-grade SS-304 steel, Teflon-coated conveyor belts, and internationally-sourced heating elements to ensure performance that lasts.
